About

Drive, skid and jump around an open world in search of huge jumps in this sixties car adventure.

Help Jim Morresong find his mojo in the desert.

It's the n'64, Jim is a promising front man yet to meet Ray and start their band. He's riding the crest of a wave but his inspiration has run dry, so he gets in his car and drives out to the desert to find his Mojo.

Can you find Mojo Risin' hidden in the desert? Can you find some peyote cactus?

Help guide and drive him on his journey to stardom in Car Jump Adventure

Impossible jumps in impossible locations in an impossible car with impossible ramps.

Can you find the peyote cactus' that will help Jim get his mojo? and then compete in the grand finale "change your perception" challenge.

Compete against your friends on the Car Jump leaderboards. Who can get to the top of the Car Jump rankings? and who can find how to open the doors of perception, finding Jim's mojo and transcending above the highest jumps?
